Why we exist

Ch4ng3 Org. exists because the earth deserves a voice. We are an independent global campaigning organization that acts to ch4ng3 attitudes and behaviour, to protect the environment and create a revolution in how we face climate change. Protecting the environment, plants and people that depend on it. Campaigning for sustainability by encouraging socially responsible actions and activities. Creating a toxic free future with safer alternatives to hazardous chemicals in today's products and manufacturing. The world needs actions. The world needs Ch4ng3!
